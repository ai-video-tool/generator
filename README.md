## What Is an AI Video Generator?

An AI video generator is a software tool powered by artificial intelligence that automatically creates videos from text prompts, scripts, images, or other inputs. Instead of manually editing scenes and adding transitions, users can input instructions, and the AI builds a structured video with visuals, voiceovers, captions, and animations.

AI video generators typically use:

- Natural Language Processing (NLP)  
- Machine Learning algorithms  
- Computer Vision  
- Text-to-Speech technology  
- Automated editing engines  

The goal is simple: **make professional video creation fast, accessible, and scalable.**
